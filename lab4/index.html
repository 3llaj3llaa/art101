How to make Sopa De Conchas

  1. Take out all of your materials
    • Knife
    • Bowl
    • Blender
    • Cutting board
    • 2 Tomatoes
    • 1 Onion
    • 1 Garlic Clove
    • 2 Sprigs of Cilantro
    • 1 Carrot
    • 2 Tablespoons of Oil
    • 4 Cups Chicken Stock
    • Box of pasta shells
    • Soup pot
    • Saucepan
    • Salt to taste
  2. Prepare the vegetables
    • Dice 2 tomatoes into quarters
    • Cut onion into quarters
    • Peel garlic clove
    • Cut the carrot into small bites
  3. Place veggies into a blender
    • Add half a cup of water and chicken stock into blender
    • Add tomatoes, onions, and garlic into blender
    • Blend until you have a smooth sauce and set aside
  4. Boil water in a large soup pot and add a pinch of salt
  5. Once the water boils, add in pasta shells
    • Wait 7 minutes or until pasta shells are soft
  6. Drain the water, leaving 4 tablespoons with the pasta
  7. In a sauce pan add the oil
    • Leave until the oil heats up
  8. Add the carrots,cilantro and sauce to the pan
  9. Pour in 4 cups of chicken stock and bring to a boil over medium heat
  10. Add in cooked pasta shells to the pot and season with salt to taste
    • Let simmer for 8-10 minutes or until the vegetables are well cooked
  11. Turn off heat and serve immediately in bowls
